Our Story

Spacious, Welcoming, Elegant

Located in Boufkran, near the Meknès-Est toll booth, Luxury Andalous offers a refined dining atmosphere that combines Moroccan tradition with international flavors.

Whether you are a traveler on the main road or a local looking for a family meal, our bright, spacious interiors provide the perfect setting for an unforgettable experience.
